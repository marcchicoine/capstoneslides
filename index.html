Adding Rhythm and Songline

I utilized a simple rhythmic sequence for each segment of choreo. I also overlayed the chant to be recited alongside the dance.

Interaction on screen

Interaction Points

Naming system for each visual marker. By walking to the specific space and saying the location name into the microphone (e.g. "EGYPT"), the user can begin to interact with the associated song, dance, and visual projection.

Users can also hover over the map on the computer console and click the highlighted names of locations in order to access the songline and dance for that space.
